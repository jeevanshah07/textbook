\begin{center}
    \textbf{\Huge Hierarchy of Functions}
\end{center}

\vspace{\stretch{1}}

\noindent We consider how fast different types of functions approach infinity. Here, assume that $c\in\mathbb{R}^+$ and $x\to\infty$.
\[...<\ln(\ln(x))<\ln(x)<x^{1/c}<x<x^c<c^x<x!<x^x<x^{x^x}<...\]
